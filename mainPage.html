Minecraft is a voxel-based game written in Java by Markus "Notch" Persson.
In 2014, Minecraft was sold to Microsoft, who currently owns and runs Mojang.
Minecraft is a sandbox game where you can play in many different gamemodes and difficulties, allowing players to build peacefully, or take on hordes of enemies and travel to another dimension to fight the final boss, the Ender Dragon.
Players can also create farms to feed themselves and any passive mobs they find. They can also choose to mine for better materials to create tools that will make their job easier. Players can also play with others, create and use their own skins, change things up with commands, create datapacks and modify the game, and play and create custom maps. They can also travel through a procedurally generated world, and find structures, including villages to trade with (and steal from) its inhabitants. Using all the materials you can gather, you can also enchant weapons and brew potions to make the player stronger.
